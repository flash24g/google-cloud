Daily Programme

05.00 A.M. - Student get up from bed.
05.50 A.M. - General prayer and meditation.
06.30 A.M. to 07.30 A.M. - Lecturer aided study hour.
07.30 A.M. to 08.30 A.M. - Break fast, student and staff served the same dishes prepared in the campus kitchen.
08.30 A.M. to 12.30 P.M. - Class Work/ Practical.
12.30 P.M. to 01.30 P.M. - Lunch- Students and staff have common lunch, Rest time/ Library.
01.30 P.M. to 02.30 P.M. - Class Work / Lecturer aided study hour.
02.30 P.M. to 05.45 P.M. - Rest time/ Self Study.
05.45 P.M. to 06.00 P.M. - Evening snacks.
06.00 P.M. to 09. 00 P.M. - Study hour under the guidance of lecturer.
09.00 P.M. to 09.30 P.M. - Dinner.
09.30 P.M. to 10.30 P.M. - Self study.
10.30 P.M. - Bed time.
